Lean Abler

On the journey of digital lean · tonytanakorn@gmail.com

Hi! I'm Tony (Tanakorn Tantanawat). I'm a researcher at National Electronics and Computer Technology Center (NECTEC), Thailand. I'm passionate about developing technologies for manufacturers to run production more efficiently.

Since management is one of the key factors affecting how efficiently manufacturers run their production, my focus is to develop technologies that enhance production management. My approach is to use "digital lean" concept, where lean management philosophy meets digital technologies, providing a powerful production management system.

The challenges on my digital lean journey are not only technical aspects of developing technologies. The most challenging part lies in understanding the targeted customers, who are small and medium-sized enterprise (SME) manufacturers. SME manufacturers pose unique constraints and requirements when adopting changes, which require special attention when designing and deploying technologies for them.

If you're feeling that my journey is somewhat the same or similar to yours, and want to chat, share experiences, or collaborate, please don't hesitate to drop me a message.
